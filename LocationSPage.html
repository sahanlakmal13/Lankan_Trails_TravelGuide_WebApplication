Sacred City of Anuradhapura, Sri Lanka

Anuradhapura is a major city in Sri Lanka. It is the capital city of North Central Province, Sri Lanka and the capital of Anuradhapura District. Anuradhapura is one of the ancient capitals of Sri Lanka, famous for its well-preserved ruins of an ancient civilization which was ruled by kings and queens.

These ruins have become a major part in the culture of Sri Lanka. Worshipping temples and visiting old ruins have also become a tourist attraction in these places.
