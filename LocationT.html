Galle Sri Lanka

Galle is a major city in sri lanka. According to the records of the 6th century traveler Cosmos Indicopolstes, Galle was mentioned as a calling town for the Levant during his voyage to Sri Lanka. Galle Fort is one of the important places in galle. Galle Fort or Dutch Fort is a fort built by the Portuguese on the southwest coast of Sri Lanka.

Not only ,Galle has an interactive beach. Unawatuna beach and jungle beach are one of them. Due to this,a large number of foreigners are currently visiting these palces in the Galle District.
